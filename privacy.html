Privacy Policy — Return Window Radar

App: Return Window Radar

Developer: ProvenMachine

Contact: inquiry@provenmachine.com

Last updated: 2026-06-26

Summary

Return Window Radar does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. Everything you enter stays on your device. There are no accounts, no servers, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking.

What data the app stores

The app lets you record purchases you want to track for returns. For each item you may enter: an item name, a store name (optional), a purchase date, a return window or exact return-by date, and a price (optional).

This information is saved only on your device, in the app's local storage (AsyncStorage). It is never sent to us or to anyone else, because the app makes no network requests of any kind.

What we collect: nothing

  • No personal data is collected or transmitted. The app has no backend and contacts no server.
  • No accounts or sign-in. You don't create an account or log in.
  • No analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs are read or shared.
  • No access to your camera, photos, files, microphone, location, contacts, calendar, health data, or notifications.

How your data is used

The data you enter is used solely to display your return windows, deadlines, statuses, and the "money still returnable" total on your device. It is not used for any other purpose.

Sharing and disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your data to anyone. Because the data never leaves your device, there is nothing for us to share.

Data retention and deletion

Your data remains on your device until you remove it. You can delete any item from within the app at any time, and uninstalling the app removes all of its locally stored data. There is no server-side copy to request deletion of.

Children's privacy

The app is suitable for all ages, does not knowingly collect any data from anyone (including children), and contains no ads or in-app purchases.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same URL with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to inquiry@provenmachine.com.
